Platform Overview

Intelligent and insightful AgTech platform

Our AIR Uzhavan, the Cloud and Mobile centric platform integrates real-time data from ground sensors and NDVI imaging from surveillance drones. Powered by AI in the cloud, it analyzes these datasets to deliver user-friendly insights directly to farmers' mobile devices. Additionally, we provide actionable solutions based on detected issues, empowering farmers to take well-informed decisions and actions with the optimized resources.

Features

AIR Uzhavan platform leverages Generative AI (Gen AI) and Digital Twins in a symbiotic manner like using our AI engine to structure inputs and synthesize outputs of digital twins, and the digital twins can provide a robust test-and-learn environment for Gen AI. Our integrated and intelligent AgTech platform using these two technologies together, the farming community can achieve exponentially more than they could by using just one to achieve the sustainable agriculture.

Precision Farming

Optimize resource utilization with AI-driven precision agriculture. Get real-time insights for crop management, soil health monitoring, and targeted interventions to maximize yield while minimizing input costs.

Smart Irrigation

Implement IoT-based irrigation systems that automatically adjust water distribution based on soil moisture, weather forecasts, and crop requirements. Save water while ensuring optimal crop growth.

Smart Storage

Reduce post-harvest losses with IoT-enabled storage solutions. Monitor temperature, humidity, and air quality in real-time while getting predictive maintenance alerts for optimal storage conditions.

Aerial Assessment

Deploy advanced drones for crop monitoring, disease detection, and yield estimation. Get high-resolution imagery and AI-powered analysis for timely decision-making and crop health management.

AI Insights

Leverage machine learning algorithms for predictive analytics, market intelligence, and personalized recommendations. Make data-driven decisions for improved farm productivity and profitability.
